Classes

What can I take in the Mac Lab?

Computer Graphix

This class is a dynamic and comprehensive program designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to excel in the field of graphic design. This course is ideal for beginners and aspiring designers seeking to explore the fundamentals of graphic design and gain proficientcy in Adobe Illustrator, a cornerstone software tool in the industry.

Digital Production

This class is a comprehensive program that introduces students to the essentials of 3D design, navigation of 3D spaces, proficiency in Fusion 360, and the art of 3D printing. This course offers hands-on experience, practical projects, and professional guidance to help students develop the skills and knowledge required for successful 3D design, modeling, and the practical application of 3D printing. Whether you're a beginner or seeking to enhance your 3D design expertise, this class is your gateway to mastering the art of 3D creation with Fusion 360 and bringing your designs to life through 3D printing.

Photography

This class is a comprehensive program designed to provide students with a solid foundation in photography techniques while equipping them with the skills to excel in image editing using Adobe Photoshop. Whether you're a novice shutterbug or a budding photographer seeking to enhance your post-processing abilities, this class offers a dynamic learning experience.

Adobe Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a renowned vector graphics software known for its precision and flexibility. It empowers designers and artists to create logos, illustrations, and scalable graphics with ease. With a wide array of tools and features, it offers exceptional control over shapes, lines, colors, and typography, making it an essential software for graphic design and visual communication. Illustrator's user-friendly interface and its ability to produce high-quality, resizable artwork have made it a go-to choice for both novices and professionals in the field. Whether crafting intricate illustrations or designing sleek corporate logos, Illustrator's capabilities are both vast and versatile.

  • Class: Computer Graphix
  • Program Type: Graphic Design

fusion 360

Fusion 360 is a versatile and advanced 3D modeling and CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software developed by Autodesk. It is widely employed by engineers, product designers, and architects for its comprehensive set of tools and capabilities, facilitating the creation of complex 3D designs and simulations. Fusion 360 stands as an essential resource for transforming innovative ideas into tangible, functional prototypes, whether for mechanical components, architectural structures, or electronic circuit boards. Its user-friendly interface and cloud-based collaboration features make it a go-to choice for professionals and students alike, fostering innovation and efficiency across various industries.

  • Class: Digital Production
  • Program Type: 3d Modeling

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Photoshop, an industry-standard image editing software, stands as a versatile and powerful tool for professionals and enthusiasts alike. With its wide array of features, it enables users to retouch photographs, create digital art, and design compelling graphics. Its user-friendly interface facilitates ease of use, while its advanced functions cater to the most demanding creative projects. Whether for photo retouching, graphic design, or digital painting, Photoshop continues to set the industry standard for creative software.

  • Class: Photography
  • Program Type: Photo Editing

Replicator+

The Replicator+ 3D printer is a top-tier, professional-grade device, admired for its precision and efficiency in producing intricate 3D objects, making it a valuable asset in various settings.

  • Class: Digital Production
  • Equipment Type: 3d Printer

Dye Sublimation Printer

The dye sublimation printer is a versatile and user-friendly device, allowing for the creation of vibrant, high-quality prints on various surfaces with ease. Its accessibility and straightforward operation make it an ideal tool for a wide range of creative projects.

  • Class: Computer Graphix
  • Equipment Type: Printer

MethodX/Formlabs

The Formlabs and Method X 3D printers are highly sophisticated devices, known for their precision and capability to create intricate objects with remarkable detail. Their use is restricted and requires special permission from Mrs. Sheehan, reflecting the need for responsible handling and expertise in operating these advanced machines.

  • Class: Digital Production
  • Equipment Type: 3d Printer
